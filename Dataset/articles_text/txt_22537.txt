Several parts of the country witnessed violent protests against the Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA) and National Citizenship Register (NRC) but there were also many who came out in support of the new law. Amid a flurry of videos doing the rounds on social media, the latest clip shows a large number of people waving saffron flags. It is being claimed that people came out on the streets of Bhilwara for supporting CAA and NRC.

India Today's Anti-Fake News War Room (AFWA) has found the viral claim to be false. The video is from the Ram Navami Shobha Yatra held in Kalaburagi, Karnataka, and has nothing to do with CAA and NRC.

Facebook page ' ' posted the video along with a caption in Hindi, the translation of which reads: In support of CAA and NRC, there is an atmosphere of 'Jai Jai Shri Ram' in Bhilwara today. Modi ji don’t step back, every Hindu is with you except for a few Jaychands.

The archived version of the post can be seen here.The post was shared more than 4,000 times at the time of filing this story.

To verify the claim, we reverse-searched the video using InVid tool. We found this video on YouTube uploaded in April 2018 along with the title Shree ram navami in kalaburagi 2018’.

In 2018 as well, a similar video -- shot from the opposite side -- went viral in the name of Ayodhya. At that time, AFWA in its investigation had found that the viral video was from the Ram Navami Shobha Yatra held in Kalaburagi.
