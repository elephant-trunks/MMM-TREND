The news of six Goa MLAs going to Brazil to watch the FIFA World Cup has created quite a stir with the opposition the Congress calling this a wasteful expenditure The trip is estimated to cost Rs 89 lakh Goa Chief Minister Manohar Parrikar was quick to respond when he said this was a study tour and that this will help in Goas preparations for the Under-17 FIFA World Cup in 2017
