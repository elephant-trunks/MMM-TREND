A disturbing photo is viral on social media which shows a mob slaughtering cow on Indian flag and burning the flag. It’s claimed to have happened in Kashmir.

Truth

This photo is 3 year Old, from Karachi, Pakistan. This was part of a protest by Pakistanis against India over Beef Ban.

A twitter handle Israel News Flash tweeted this image on 7th April 2015. It wrote

“This is how people in Pakistan protest against the #BeefBan in India!”



You can see the Hoarding in the background with an advertisement of “Digger” which is a Pakistani footwear company
