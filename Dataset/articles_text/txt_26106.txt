On social media, many users are claiming that former Cricketer Sourav Ganguly has resigned from the position of President of BCCI.

People are sharing such images, a user on Facebook writes, “Saurabh Ganguly resigned from the post of BCCI President.” with the image that states the above mentioned in Hindi.

Another user on Facebook shared a screenshot from ABP Live which states that Sourav Ganguly is resigning as the president of BCCI as he is planning to start something that will help a lot of people.

Fact Check:

After a tweet by Sourav that states, “ I am planning to strat something that I feel will propaby help a lot of people .” people thought that he is resigning from the position of President of BCCI.

But, in that tweet there was no mention of his resigning from his position.

Looking at all the viral news about this matter ANI posted from its official account Denying the resign of Sourav Ganguly.

ANI writes, “Sourav Ganguly has not resigned as the president of BCCI: Jay Shah, BCCI Secretary to ANI.”

Sourav Ganguly has not resigned as the president of BCCI: Jay Shah, BCCI Secretary to ANI pic.twitter.com/C2O3r550aL — ANI (@ANI) June 1, 2022

Conclusion:

Hence, the viral news of Sourav Ganguly resining from the position of BCCI president is fake.
