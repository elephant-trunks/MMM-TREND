A screengrab of a tweet of Aaj Tak is going viral on the internet. Further, on the screengrab it is written, “CBI was not able to find single evidence against Gandhi Family, BJP apologised to Gandhi family in the court.”

Further, sharing the screengrab Facebook user, Pitamber Mishra wrote the caption, “Truth may be disturbed but not defeated. After all!! He has to come, it will take some time..”

(सत्य परेशान हो सकता है पर पराजित नहीं।आखिर !!उसे आना है जरा देर लगेगी।।)

Similarly, many social media users have shared this screengrab on their social media handles.

Factcheck

In our factcheck analysis we checked the verified page of Aajtak to find the tweet. We did not find any such tweet. But, we found a post slamming these rumours. Furthermore, in the tweet it was written, “#FactCheck | The image of this fake tweet is being circulated in the name of Aaj Tak’s tweet.”

Conclusion

Since, Aajtak itself in a post, have clarified that the viral screengrab is fake. Therefore, there is no such news and the claims of the social media users are misleading.
