A video of singer and BJP’s Lok Sabha MP Hans Raj Hans singing 'Raghupati Raghava Raja Ram' a bhajan popularised by Mahatma Gandhi - is viral on social media with the claim that the line "Ishwar Allah Tero Naam" (God and Allah are your names) has been removed from the bhajan by the Narendra Modi government. The claim also says this line has been replaced by “Jai Shree Ram, Sita Ram”.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu can also be seen in the video.

The caption to one such post reads, “Finally "Ishwar Allah tero naam" is removed and replaced by “Jai Shree Ram, Sita Ram" in Raghupati Raghav Raja ram. Dhanyawad @narendramodi ji”

India Today Anti Fake News War Room (AFWA) has found that the video in question is clipped. In a longer version of the video, we can hear Hans Raj Hans singing the line "Ishwar Allah Tero Naam".
