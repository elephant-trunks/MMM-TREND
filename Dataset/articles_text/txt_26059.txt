A video of PM Australia, Tonny Abbott Praising Narendra Modi is getting viral on social media.

In the video, he is referring to Modi as a rapturously received Prime Minister of the country.

In the entire video, he can be heard praising Narendra Modi and the democratic system of India. People are sharing the video with the claim that PM Modi is being praised by the Australian PM.

Many users on Facebook shared the video with the caption that reads, “When Australian PM met Modi ji”

Links can be seen here and here.

Fact Check:

We at DFRAC reverse searched the video by using specific keywords and found the same video on the official YouTube channel of Narendra Modi.

The video was exactly the same, but the year in which it was posted was 2014. The video was shared with the caption, “PM Modi at the Australian Parliament in Canberra.”

Conclusion:

The video shared by the social media users is true, but it is not of recent times but from the year 2014.

Claim Review: A video of PM Australia, Tonny Abbott Praising Narendra Modi is getting viral on social media.

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact check : misleading
