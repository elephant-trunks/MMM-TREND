Lakhs of students who appeared in the class term 1 examination for class 10 and class 12 are eagerly waiting for their results. Further, many reports are making speculations that CBSE will declare the results this week itself. In the same vein, a circular is getting viral on the internet. The circular claims that it will announce the class 12th result will be today only. Also, this circular has a sign of Anurag Tripathi, secretary of CBSE.

Consequently, this news has created a lot of confusion and embranglement among the students and their family members who are long waiting for the results.

FactCheck

On our fact check analysis we found that this circular is fake and baseless. Further, we also found that CBSE has not yet announced an official date for the declaration of the result of class 12th boards.

Moreover, we found a tweet on its official handle.

Therefore, from the above tweet, it is clear that the class 12th result will not be announced today.

Conclusion

Hence, our fact checks analysis proved that the circular is fake and misleading. Therefore, DFRAC request student only to check updates regarding the result or curriculum on the official website of CBSE.
