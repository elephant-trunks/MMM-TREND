As the dates for the assembly elections in Uttar Pradesh are approaching, the scope of spreading rumors is also increasing. Meanwhile, a big news came out. In which it was claimed that Maulana Tauqir Raza’s daughter-in-law Nida Khan has joined BJP.

Many big media houses covered this news and wrote that Maulana Tauqir Raza’s daughter-in-law Nida Khan joined BJP in Lucknow. However, the picture used in the news is not of Nida Khan.

Fact Check

Farhat Naqvi

On checking the above picture, we found that the picture attached with the news is of Farhat Naqvi, sister of Union Minority Affairs Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi. Who is the president of an organization named Mera Haq Foundation. She helps Muslim women in matters related to triple talaq.

Nida Khan

On the other hand, Nida Khan is not the daughter-in-law of Maulana Tauqeer Raza but the daughter-in-law of his brother Maulana Anjum Mian. She was married to Sheeran Raza Khan, the son of Maulana Anjum Mian. Nida’s husband Sheeran divorced her on 5 February 2016.

Conclusion

It is concluded that the viral image is fake as this picture is not of Nida Khan but of Farhat Naqvi.
