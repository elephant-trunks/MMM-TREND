After the Karauli riots, many cases of violence were seen in different cities. Moreover, social media is flooded with hateful and derogatory posts. Meanwhile, a video related to the Karauli violence is going viral. Further, sharing the video @Metpco wrote the caption, “Rajasthan:- Terrorists roaming freely after burning Karauli. Speaking to Hindus, ‘We have lit 4 vehicles in our area, our people have created so much panic in our area. So much fear is enough for your people, Hindus.”

Similarly many users have shared this video on their handles.

Fact Check

On reverse image search, we found the same post on the account named Gangs Of Greno. But, this post was posted on 1st May, 2020. Further, the caption of the video was, “Your fear is too much brother.. #Hyderabad.”

Further, we also found this video on the account of Indore Youth. And, it was posted on 10th May,2022. The caption of the video was, “This much fear is enough for you people’. Fearing the people of other community in fanatic gestures.”

Hence, the video is old and not related to recent Karauli violence.
