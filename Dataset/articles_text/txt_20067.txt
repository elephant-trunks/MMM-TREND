After the split up of the alliance of BJP and JDU in Bihar, once again the grand alliance government has been formed. In which Nitish Kumar has become the Chief Minister and Tejashwi Yadav has become the Deputy Chief Minister.

Meanwhile, a video is going viral on social media. A train full of crowds can be seen in the video. It was claimed that this train is full of Bangladeshis and Rohingyas.

The user wrote in his tweet -“#BiharPoliticalcrisis after Lalu Prasad Yadav and #नीतीश Kumar joined hands together, happy Bangladeshis and Rohingyas coming to Bihar. #MuradMerali #AnjanaOmKashyap #BiharPolitics #TejasviYadav #JDU_RJD #palturam #बिहार #पलटूराम

Fact check:

To check the viral video, we first converted the video into a keyframe with the help of the InVID tool and then did a reverse search image. During this ,we found one such video on YouTube.

The video was uploaded on May 1 on a Bangladesh-based channel called BD Train Express. Giving information about the train seen in the video, it was said that this train was 47 Up Dewanganj. Also, the place was said to be Balashpur overpass Mayman singh.f

Apart from this, we also received a report from Dail Mail . In which we found screenshots of the video. The report mentioned, thousands of people travelled in Front, side and roof of the train to celebrate Eid in Bangladesh.

Conclusion:

Viral video is misleading.
