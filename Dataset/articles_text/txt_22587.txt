Conclusion

Palestinian freelance journalist Muath Amarneh recently lost an eye while covering a protest in the West Bank. Donning eye-patches, journalists and activists worldwide started a social media campaign in solidarity with Amarneh.

These reports can be read here and here .

But we could not find any credible news report or image related to these international soccer stars showing solidarity with the people of Gaza by covering an eye. Thus we can conclude that the images used in the viral post are photoshopped.
