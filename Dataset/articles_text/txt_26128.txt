Fact Check:

To find the reality of the viral image, we reverse searched the image and found that the the person in the viral picture is not Goldy Brar who murdered Mose Wala.

We found the image of the murdered Goldy Brar on a news report by India Today. Which do not at all looks like the person in the viral image.

So, to get more information about the person seen in the viral picture, we started searching with some related keywords on Google. After which we found an article published on Aajtak’s website related to the case. According to the article, the name of the person seen in the viral picture is also Goldie Brar, but it is not Goldy, who is accused of the murder of singer Musewala, but Goldy Brar of Fazilka.

Conclusion:

The viral picture of Bhagwant Mann with Goldie Brar of Fazilka is not the Goldie Brar who have murdered Sidhu Moose Wala.
