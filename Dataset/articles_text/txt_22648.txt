This is the time of Garba! Several videos of Garba are doing rounds on social media. Meanwhile, a video of an old woman dancing to a tune has gone viral with the claim that this is Prime Minister Narendra Modi's mother, Hira ben.

The archived version is saved here.

India Today Anti Fake News War Room (AFWA) found that this is a wrong claim. The old woman in the viral video is not PM Modi's mother Hira Ben.

advertisement

A Facebook page "Sushma Swaraj Ji Rashtrapati ho" posted this video which has been shared by more than 1,200 people till the time of writing this article.

Simple keyword search with the help of words "Narendra Modi's mother dancing", we found this viral video is available on the internet since 2017.

This video was uploaded on YouTube on September 30, 2017 by one Ketan Chheda with the caption "Garba Fever".

On October 20, 2017, Lieutenant Governor Puducherry Kiran Bedi had tweeted this video claiming that she was Narendra Modi's mother dancing with the spirit of Deepavali.

Several fact checkers like "Boom Live" along with other mainstream media had done story on Kiran Bedi's gaffe. Kiran Bedi later clarified the mistake in another tweet.

Close comparison with the images of the woman in viral video with Hira Ben, Modi's mother confirms the difference in their faccial appearances.
