He tweeted from his account, “Not Ukraine, these are photos of Unnao and Farrukhabad of Buldozerland.”

Fact Check:

To know the authenticity of the viral pictures we reverse searched different images.

We found the same pictures in the report of Amaj Ujala with the headline, “encroachment drive launched.” From the same report, we found that the pictures are from Unnao, but from the year 2016.

Conclusion:

After the Fact Check by DFRAC we found that the pictures shared by Salman Khurshid is Misleading as the viral pictures are from the year 2016.
