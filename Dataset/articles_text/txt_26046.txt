A video is going viral on social media with the claim that a terrorist was caught at a metro station. Further, along with the video a Facebook user, Laxman Jaat Palwal claimed that a terrorist was caught at the Faridabad Metro Station.

Similarly, many other users have shared this video with the same claim.

Fact Check

Using InVID tool on the different frames of the video, we found the viral video on YouTube. The video was uploaded on the YouTube channel of Faridabad News on 25th June, 2022. And, the title of the video is, “Terrorist caught on Faridabad Metro Station is Wrong, It’s part of CISF Mock Drill, यह है सच्चाई.”

Furthermore, while keyword search we found a tweet on the official account of the Faridabad Police which clarify that the viral video is of a Mock drill. And, it is written in the tweet, “Before #सच_जानो #फिर_लिखो This video is a part of mock drill conducted by CISF. Know the truth, don’t spread rumours. #नीम_हकीम Khatra-e-Jaan.”

Conclusion

Therefore, the viral video is part of a Mock drill. Hence, the claims of the social media users are misleading.
