Fact Check:

We at DFRAC reverse searched the video to know the truth behind stone pelting by a group of Muslim people.

We found the same video covered under many news sites like India Today, ETV Bharat under the caption, “a group of Muslims were pelting stones at a train after its horn interrupted their prayers.”

And, “Chennai students pelt stones at train amid scuffle as passengers panic.”

The viral video of the stone-pelting shows a clash between two groups of college students that took place in Chennai a few days back.

Conclusion:

The viral video is not of any communal riots, but was a clash between two groups of collage students.
