The wedding of famous Bollywood Actor Salman Khan has always been a buzz in India. This buzz got more fire when recently a picture was shared on social media sites showcasing Sonakshi Sinha (Bollywood actress) and Salman Khan where Salman is seen dressed in a white sherwani and Sonakshi is in a red bridal lehenga as a married couple. People all over the internet are talking about their secret marriage.

An image was shared by Quick Times states, “Salman Khan secretly marriage With Sonakshi Sinha.”

Fact Check:

By only looking at the picture it can be said that the picture is morphed and not a real picture.

Many media houses like The Tribune, etc have published this news and claimed it as fake news.

Sonakshi too has spoken on this picture, asking people, “are you dumb that you cannot tell the difference between a real and morphed image.”

Conclusion:

The image getting viral is morphed image and not a real one.

Claim Review: A viral picture suggesting Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha as married.

Claimed by: Social media users.

Fact Check: fake.
