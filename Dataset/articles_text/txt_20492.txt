Picture shared on social media on March 7 and 8, 2022, amidst Ukraine’s attack on Russia, claimed that Ukraine destroyed a Russian helicopter.

Sharing the picture, it was captioned that “AFU report may be reviewed on the destruction of a #Russian helicopter near #Vyshgorod.”

Fact Check:

On reverse-image of the viral image, we found that this image is of February 24, 2022. The day the Russian invasion of Ukraine began.

This picture can be seen in the New York Post and Daily Mail report on the Russia-Ukraine conflict published on February 24.

Hence the viral picture has been found to be misleading.

Conclusion:

The claim is misleading.

Claim review: Ukraine destroys a #Russian helicopter near #Vyshgorod

Claim by: Bekeris Povilas

Fact check: misleading
