A piece of news is getting viral on the internet that veteran actor Dharmendra passes away. Further, many fans are paying tributes to him on social media by sharing emotional posts. Fact Check

On our fact check analysis we many reports in which Sunny Deol, Bobby Deol dismiss reports of Dharmendra’s deteriorating health.

Conclusion

Hence, the news of Bollywood actor’s death is mere rumours.
