A picture is doing rounds on the internet on the internet with the claim that Muslim Women in foreign are supporting Nupur Sharma. In the picture, a Hijab- clad woman is holding a placard in which it is written, “’कोर्ट/मदरसा/मौलवी बताएं आखिर ‘आयशा’ की शादी किस उम्र में हुई? मुझे नूपुर शर्मा को गलत साबित करना है.’ लड़की के पीछे कुछ दूसरे लोग भी नजर आ रहे हैं”

English Translation – “Tell ‘Court / Madrasa / Maulvi At what age did ‘Ayesha’ got married? I have to prove Nupur Sharma wrong. Some other people are also seen behind the girl.”

Similarly, many other users have shared this picture with a similar claim.

Fact Check

On a simple keyword search, we found no such news. Further, on reverse image search, we found the picture of the same Hijab clad woman in several articles including, Dawn, Aljazeera and The Tribune.

Furthermore, to verify that this picture, we took the help of fotoforensic.

Conclusion

Therefore, DFRAC fact check proves that the viral image is doctors. The content of the placard is different on the original image. Hence, the piece of news attached to the picture that foreign women coming in support of Nupur Sharma is fake.
