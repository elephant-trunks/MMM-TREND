A video is widely being shared on social media with a claim that it shows a copper plate excavated from Ayodhya. The caption claims that the copper plate is from the Buddhist era and shows the legacy of Buddhism in Ayodhya.

The video is shared with a caption: “अयोध्या की खुदाई में बौद्ध काल का ताम्रपत्र मिला जो को पाली भाषा में है, बौद्ध धम्म की धरोहर होने का सबसे बड़ा सबूत।अब भी कोई शक रह गया।” (English translation: “In the excavation of Ayodhya, a copper plate of the Buddhist period was found, which is in Pali language — the biggest evidence of Ayodhya being the heritage of Buddhism. Still, is there any doubt?”
