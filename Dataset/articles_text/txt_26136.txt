A collage of images of Priyanka Chopra Jones is going viral. Some captions have been written on this viral photo. In this caption, an objectionable statement has been made about lord Shiva, the aradhya of the Hindu society.

On Twitter, a user named Rajiv Kumar Maurya wrote- “The exact answer of Priyanka Chopra, who could not even recognize her son with three eyes on her forehead, how will her devotees be able to identify what the real thing is? ”(translates Hindi)

The same graphical photo has been posted on Facebook by a user named Birendra Bai Pass.

Fact Check:

We searched on Google for a fact check of this news. On searching, we found a report published on March 1, 2022 on the website of ‘Indian Express’. The report said that Priyanka Chopra and her husband Nick Jonas offered prayers to Lord Shiva on the day of Mahashivratri at their home in the US. Many photos of Pooja were also shared by Priyanka Chopra on the status of her Instagram account.

From this report, it is clear that Priyanka Chopra worships Lord Shiva, then the question arises whether Priyanka Chopra has really made any such statement about Lord Shiva. To investigate this, we did a reverse search to find out the truth of his photos with graphical posters. After which we got a video published on the YouTube channel of ‘Movie Talkies’ on June 9, 2016.

The thumbnail of this video shows the same photo of Priyanka Gandhi, which is going viral with graphical content. According to this news, Priyanka Chopra got angry with the journalists at the Mumbai airport.

Conclusion:

Our fact check is proving that Priyanka Chopra has not made an objectionable statement about Lord Shiva. His photoshopped statement is being made viral by social media users. Therefore, the claim of social media users is misleading.

Claim Review-Priyanka Chopra made an objectionable statement about Lord Shiva

Claimed by – Social Media Users

Fact Check – False and Misleading.
