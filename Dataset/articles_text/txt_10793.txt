Teesta Setalvad is an activist, journalist and a Padma Shri recipient. She is the secretary of the NGO called Citizens for Justice and Peace that works for the victims of 2002 riots. In June 2002, she had testified against the then CM of Gujarat Narendra Modi for his role in the 2002 Gujarat riots at the United States Commission on International Religious Freedom. Her NGO and the widow of late Congress MP Ehsan Jafri, Zakia Jafri had filed a petition against Narendra Modi and others alleging a larger conspiracy during the riots. On June 24 this year, the Supreme Court dismissed these allegations and gave a clean chit to Narendra Modi, the Prime Minister of India.
