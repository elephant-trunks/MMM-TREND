A picture of Rahul Gandhi is going viral on the internet again. In the Picture, Rahul Gandhi is with a girl. Further, social media users are sharing this viral photo with the claim that she is the same girl who was in the Karnataka hijab Row controversy. Meanwhile, sharing the picture, Ankita Singh wrote in the caption which can be nearly translated into English, “This is the girl from Karnataka, understood now, The whole game belongs to Congress!!

https://twitter.com/Ankita_107/status/1491712102939066373

Similarly, Dinesh wrote, “This is the girl from Karnataka, understand now! The whole game belongs to Congress!! ?

The real root of the hijab game!!”

Likewise,, many other social media users shared this picture. Apparently, by sharing this picture with these captions, they are trying to, convey that somewhere hands of Rahul Gandhi or Congres are involved in the ongoing Karnataka Hijab row protest.

FactCheck

On our FactCheck analysis, our team first tried to decode who real identity of the girl in the picture. Consequently, we found out that she is Amba Prasad, “MLA, Barkagaon, Jharkhand Legislative Assembly.”

Moreover, Amba Prasad herself took to her official Twitter account to quash these claims. She wrote, “This is me @INCIndia Barkagaon MLA from the party. An attempt is made to spread discord by calling this photo a girl in Karnataka’s burqa. So, let me tell you that everyone in our party has respect and rights. Be it saffron clothes or hijab. @JharkhandPolice Please take action against those who comment on such tweets. https://twitter.com/Trishul_Achuk/status/1491467868923314178…”

Conclusion

Therefore, it is clear that the girl in the viral video is not related to the ongoing Hijab row protest in Karnataka. But, some users are sharing this picture to mislead common people.
