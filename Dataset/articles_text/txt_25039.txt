No, Prime Minister is not holding an umbrella with Jio‚Äôs brand logo during the 2021 Monsoon session‚Äôs press conference.

A press release of the monsoon session of parliament 2021 published on the official website of the Press Information Bureau featured the following images.

In all three images, it is visible that the prime minister is holding an ordinary, dark-grey umbrella over his head and not the one with a Jio logo, as depicted in the viral image.

Furthermore, we found a link to the following video uploaded on the official YouTube channel of PM Modi.

In the video at 2:13, the camera starts zooming out and reveals the umbrella that, quite evidently, is not the one with Jio branding. In addition to that, changing the umbrella mid-conference is out of the question since the video evidence is a single shot with no cuts.

Now, we ran a reverse image search to find the original, unedited copy of the particular photo that went viral. We found the viral image in the following article published by a website named News Today.

Also, we found that a Twitter user also tweeted the unedited picture as a reply to one such viral post.

Hence, we can conclude that the image is completely fake. PM Modi did not hold an umbrella with Jio branding while addressing a press conference.
