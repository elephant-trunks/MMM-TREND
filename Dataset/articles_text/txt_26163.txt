A misleading information is going viral on social media. Social media users are sharing this information claiming that actor Arvind Trivedi, who played Ravana in the famous Indian television show ‘ Ramayan ‘ , has passed away. Further, users are also paying tribute to him by sharing this news.

A user wrote on Twitter – ” Arvind Trivedi” the brilliant artist who played the role of Ravana in the popular serial “Ramayana” is no more. A pillar of knowledge and devotion has fallen. Many God give him a place at his feet. Om shanti!

At the same time, a user wrote on Facebook – ” Sat Sat Naman to Ram devotee Shri Arvind Trivedi ji who played the role of Ravana in Ramayana serial. Tribute!

At the same time, there are many such posts regarding his death, on Facebook and Twitter.

Fact Check:

To verify the veracity of the claim that is going viral on social media, we first searched on Google. A link of a news of ‘ Amar Ujala ‘ newspaper was found on Google . Its title was- ” Ravan’s actor Arvind Trivedi is no more , died at the age of 83 ” . This news was published by Amar Ujala on 6 October 2021.

Apart from this, we found news published on 6th October 2021 of his demise on many news websites.

While sharing the post on 6 October 2021, another user wrote – ” The news of the death of Shri Arvind Trivedi ji, who left an indelible mark in the hearts of crores of people in the character of Ravana in the popular serial “Ramayana”, is extremely sad. May Lord Shri Ram give him a place at his feet and give strength to the bereaved family. ” Peace ”

Conclusion:

Arvind Trivedi passed away on 6 October 2021. But, social media users are posting information related to his death is in a misleading manner.
