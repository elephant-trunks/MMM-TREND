Fact Check

Om google reverse image search, we found a 1 minute and 33 minutes video on Youtube channel of VOXKOMM International. At 52 seconds we can see a man climbing on a Chimney and uprooting the Ukrainina flag. Then, he hoisted the Soviet Union Flag. But, the point worthy to note here is that this video with the caption, “Raising the Soviet flag on Donbass” was uploaded on March 4, 2015.

Conclusion

Hence, the viral video is not of Mariupol. Users are sharing a old video with the false claim.
