FactCheck

After our research analysis, we found a report from the Ukrainian National News(UNN). This report was published on March 23, 2016. Further, the headline of the report is A series of photos “Children of War” was published by the Ministry of Defense.

Further, we found many other old reports of 2016. Hence, the viral image is an old image and users are sharing it misleadingly.

Conclusion

Therefore, the viral image is of misleading. The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine published this image along with photos of children of war on its Facebook page. It is not related to the ongoing conflict between Ukraine and Russia.
