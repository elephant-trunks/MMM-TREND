Ukraine-Russia war has been continuing for 2 weeks now. Many lives have been lost during airstrikes and attacks from the Russian Army. US President Biden has announced to ban the import of Diamond, Vodka and seafood from Russia as a symbol of aggression. Meanwhile, a short click is going viral where users are claiming that it represents the situation of Ukraine in the war.

Ukraine Russia War 24×7 News Updates posted a video of a small girl, where the person is asking the girl where her dad is and have she had lunch. The video was posted on March 2, 2022. In his caption, he depicts the situation of Ukraine after the war.

Other users also posted the video and claims to be the present situation of Ukraine.

Fact check

After researching we got to know that the video is from 2017 and has no links with the Ukraine-Russia war. Humanity Lives On posted the video as the depiction of Humanity is Power. In the video, the same girl is present who is present in the viral video.

Another proof

Preemptive Love is claimed that the girl’s name is Malak and she has survived the war with ISIS. Wherein, she lost her family, therefore the viral video is not from the Ukraine war situation. Instead, it is from the ISIS war.

Conclusion: Hence proving that the claim is misleading.
