Fact Check

After doing a reverse search analysis by our team, we found out this video to be misleading and old. This video was shared by various media houses back in 2018.

AajTak also published a report sharing this video on Jan 8, 2018 with a title тАШMadhya Pradesh: Oath administered to children, will not vote for BJP.тАЩ
