Fact Check:

By simply looking at the video one could tell that the video is morphed as no words and voiceover are syncing in together.

We reverse searched too to take out the real video of the Woman Constable and her speech and found the same video on a Youtube channel of Punjab Kesari. The policewoman here in the video is complaining about her higher official in front of the media.

The same news was covered by The Times of India under the headline, “Woman Constable alleges harassment in mainpuri.”

Also, we find out the video is five years old .

Conclusion:

The video is being spread with the fake claim. Also, the video is evidently morphed in nature.
