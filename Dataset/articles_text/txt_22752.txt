In this ground report, India Today's Ashutosh Mishra details the escalating violence in Bangladesh following the death of Inquilab Manch leader Sharif Usman Hadi. Mishra states, 'the whole nation is now burning,' describing how mobs have targeted Awami League properties and media houses. The report highlights the attack on the Assistant High Commission of India in Chittagong, where officers were 'stone-pelted by the mobsters.' Mishra notes the 'lawlessness' and the failure of security agencies to contain the unrest ahead of the February elections.
