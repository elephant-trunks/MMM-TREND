Multiple media outlets published an agency story which claims that hundreds of sheep in Southern Ireland went on a week-long uncontrollable sex drive after they drank water from Ringaskiddy harbour where drug manufacturer Pfizer had accidentally spilled tonnes of Viagra.

The bizarre story, a news feed by Indo-Asian News Service, was picked by major mainstream media like hot cake.

advertisement

India Today Anti Fake News War Room (AFWA) has found this to be a satirical article originally taken from an international website known for publishing fictional stories.

The story was picked by several news websites such as News 18, Outlook and Free Press Journal. The archived versions can be seen here, here, a nd here.

Some news websites such as The Times Of India and Times Now later removed the articles.

The viral article was originally taken from a satirical website called World News Daily Report (WDNR) whose tagline says where facts don’t matter.

The Disclaimer section of the website clearly mentions that all articles are fictional in nature.

This piece of information has not been published in any mainstream Irish media. Pfizer has also denied the WDNR report about viagra contamination. This news report can be seen here.
