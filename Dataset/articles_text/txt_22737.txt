In this special report, the Unnao rape survivor speaks exclusively to India Today after meeting Leader of Opposition Rahul Gandhi and Sonia Gandhi at 10 Janpath. The survivor, who was dragged from India Gate by police while protesting the Delhi High Court’s suspension of former BJP MLA Kuldeep Singh Sengar’s life sentence, revealed that the Gandhis were ‘moved to tears’ by her ordeal. She has demanded that her family be relocated to a Congress-ruled state, citing grave safety concerns and fears that Sengar ‘can get us killed anywhere’. The survivor also expressed her desire to meet the Prime Minister and President. The report also covers the political controversy sparked by UP Minister Om Prakash Rajbhar’s insensitive remarks mocking the survivor.
