Fact Check

While watching the video closely, BOOM saw one of the bystanders in the crowd wearing a T-shirt with Pakistani flag imprinted on it.





BOOM found that most of the Twitter users who had tweeted the video, had reshared the video from the Twitter handle of Ahmad Waqass Goraya, who had tweeted the same video with an Urdu caption on May 21.

The Urdu caption, when translated to English, reads 'Maulvi was caught raping a five-year-old girl. The locals shaved their beards and eyebrows'.

Goraya is a Pakistani blogger and activist living in forced exile in the Netherlands.
