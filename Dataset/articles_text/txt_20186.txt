Factcheck

On keyword search, we found the same video on YouTube. The title of the video is , “Ahead of Bihar election, Nitish Kumar’s old video calling BJP ‘Badka Jhoota Party’ goes viral.” It is noteworthy that the video was uploaded on October 19, 2020.

Conclusion
