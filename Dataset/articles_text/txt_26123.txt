A picture of Vanathi Srinivasan, a member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and a lawyer is going viral on social media sites in which she is seen holding the poster that says #GoBackModi.

In front of the Adiyogi statue, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

People all over the internet are posting her picture with the caption, “We stand with Vanathi #GoBack_Modi #GoBackModi

Fact Check:

We reverse searched the image to find its reality. We found the same image, along with some other images on the official Twitter account of Vanathi Srinivasan.

In reality, the poster that she was holding says, “Today, the Prime Minister, who made a record of paying Rs 100 crore in vaccinations in front of the Coimbatore Adiyogi statue.@narendramodi The state president Mr. at the youth team event to thank them. @annamalai_k @VinojBJP @apmbjp attended with them #100CroreVaccination.”

Conclusion:

The picture is getting viral with the misleading claim as no #GoBackModi was written on the poster held by vanathi.

Claim Review: “#GoBackModi” sign held by Vanathi Srinivasan

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact Check: misleading.
