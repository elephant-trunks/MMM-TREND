As we all know the demise of Lata Mangeshkar has left a feeling of sorrow in the heart of our Nation. Lata Ji left us on 6th February 2022 at the age of 92, she will be known among the best singers of India also called ‘Swar Kokila’. People around the nation mourned her death on social media and a video is getting viral claiming that it was the last video of Lata Mangeshkar before her death. In the video, Lata Ji is greeting the audience and sharing her sweet journey with RD Burman.JiMangeshkar

Fact Check

After searching the video on Lata Ji’s Facebook profile we didn’t find any such videos on 4th February 2022 as claimed. However, we found a post where she went live on the death anniversary of RD Burman and there you can see the same clip of viral video. The live video was posted from her Facebook account on 4th January 2017.

Conclusion- Hence claiming that the video going viral is fake and misleading.
