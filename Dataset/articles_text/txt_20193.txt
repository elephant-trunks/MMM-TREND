A viral video of Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut’s interview is going viral in which he is seen crying.

People are claiming that he is crying in the interview as the interviewer asked about the political crisis in the party.

Many social media users are uploading this video somewhat sarcastically with the same caption which states, “#भक्तो रुला दिया ना नॉटी को” which translates to (Bhakts made the naughty cry”)

Same post was seen on twitter with similar caption.

https://twitter.com/Narpats62770513/status/1540022984710139904?s=20&t=s55D3BkvqKJY05kz6LsGDA

Fact Check:

DFRAC looked for the recent interviews of Sanjay Raut and found the one on the official YouTube channel of AajTak.

We could not find Sanjay Raut crying in the original video, the viral video is the result of a Snapchat filter which is famous among gen z. The trend is viral these days any many such morphed can be seen on social media sites of different personalities.

Likewise, the viral video of Sanjay Raut is shown frowning using the crying filter.

Conclusion:

The Raut’s face is not frowned in the original video found on Aaj Tak’s YouTube channel and has been added to the viral video using a Snapchat filter.

Claim Review: A viral video of Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut’s interview is going viral in which he is seen crying.

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact Check: misleading
