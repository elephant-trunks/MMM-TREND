Claim: Viral images posted by a Facebook user Chenna Keshav shows a little girl with a swollen abdomen. It is being claimed that the girl is just 11 years old Islamic bride and is already pregnant.

Fact Check: On the reverse search, India Today’s Anti Fake News War Room (AFWA) found that the girl is 8-year-old Roya Jan from Afghanistan. She was suffering from thalassemia which resulted in enlarged liver and spleen. Roya is receiving treatment in Iran’s Dr. Sheikh Hospital.
