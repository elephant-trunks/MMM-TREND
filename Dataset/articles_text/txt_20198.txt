A video of the Mermaid is widely shared on social media sites. It is claimed that the real mermaid has been sighted in the sea.

A Facebook user writes, “The real mermaid found in the sea.”(Translates Hindi)

Fact Check:

To check the truth behind the viral video of mermaid we searched the video using specific keyframes and found the same video on the YouTube channel of JJPD productions dated 16th February 2021. In the description box, it is clearly mentioned that the video is made purely for entertainment purpose.

Conclusion:

This is clear from the above fact check that the claim from social media users is misleading.

Claim Review: the reality behind the viral video of a Mermaid.

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact Check: misleading
