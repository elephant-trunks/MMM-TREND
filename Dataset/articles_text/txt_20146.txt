A video is going viral on social media in which a woman is offering prayers in the temple. Social media users are claiming that this woman is the wife of the King of Dubai and she is worshiping without a burqa in a temple in Tamil Nadu.

Further, sharing the video, a user named Chowkidar Ravichiruvolu wrote – “She is d wife of d King of Dubai supposed to be d head of Islam, on a business trip to Chennai n she visited Golden Temple in ShreePuram of TN No Burkha no abuse of Kaphirs. She wore thilak performed abhishekham to Goddess Lakshmi. Hear wht she is saying about visiting temples”

Fact Check:

On our investigation of the the viral video. Firstly, we visited the profile of user chowkidar Ravichiruvolu (@Ravichiruvolu1 ) who posted the video and checked his posts. Meanwhile, we got a reply from Hend FQ ( @LadyVelvet_HFQ ).

In the reply, Hend FQ wrote – “ I am not the wife of the King of Dubai. The information shared is incorrect. I came back from India with wonderful memories & I remain true to my words shared that I was humbled by the generosity & kindness of the Indians shown onto me. I’m saddened with the current Islamophobia.”
