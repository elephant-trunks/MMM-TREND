Renowned author of Kaafir’s Love, Hundred lives for U, Predator, Edge Of Machete, Abhisar Sharma is getting light on the internet. After one of his Tweets got viral. In the tweet, it is written that, if Yogi Aditya Nath will again the UP Elections then he will shift from Noida to Sheik Sarai Delhi and there he will put a stall of eggs and omelet. Sharing the screenshot of his post, @BellamSwathi asked sarcastically when will Abhisar Ji go to start an egg omelet stall in Delhi?

Fact Check

On our fact check analysis we found no such tweet by Abhisar. Further, in the viral screenshot, the timing of the tweet is shown as 11:42 P.M, 4th February. However, we found Abhisar’s other tweet done at the same time. He wrote in the tweet, “Happy Birthday @rollgandhi_Brother. Keep smiling, keep laughing. Keep your spirits high and continue your search for bead in Noida.”

Happy birthday @RoflGandhi_ bhai. Haste rahiye, hasaate rahiye. Hausla buland rakhen aur Noida me Manka kee talaash jaaree rakhen . — Abhisar Sharma (@abhisar_sharma) February 4, 2022

Conclusion

Hence, from our fact check analysis we found that, the viral screenshot of the tweet of Abhisar Sharma is fake.
