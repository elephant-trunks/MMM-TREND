Fact Check-

We started our investigation by running a keyword search on Google to find relevant news report on the US Military place air crash in the Farah Province. Farah is one of the provinces in Afghanistan location next to Iran. We could not find any relevant news report which stated about the air crash killing hundreds of citizens and US Military. It seemed very obvious that if such a major crash had taken place recently, international as well as local media is ought to report it. However, we could not find any credible media house reporting about this crash in Afghanistan recently.

Moving on to the image of the airplane on fire, we ran a reverse image search on Google which led us to the website of Alamy stock images who stated that this image is 13 years old. According to the website this image was taken on 7 July 2008. The image was taken in Baghdad, Iraq after the C-130 airplane made an emergency landing in a field north of Baghdad International Airport. The Explosive Ordinance Disposal team was using a series of controlled detonations designed to divide the airplane into smaller pieces so it could be easily moved.
