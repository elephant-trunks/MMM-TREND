Truth The above pictures shared are from the building of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML) and not the Congress office in Wayanad.

In the above picture, the symbol on the top left corner is the ladder. It is the party symbol of the Indian Union Muslim League office in Kerala. Below is the picture of the President of the Kerala state committee of the Indian Union Muslim League. It is the picture of Syed Muhammedali Shihab Thangal. Thangal was the party President of the IUML until he died in 2009.
