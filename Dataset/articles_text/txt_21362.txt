Prime Minister Narendra Modi attended the Christmas morning service at the Cathedral Church of the Redemption in New Delhi, joining a large congregation for prayers and carols. During the service, the Bishop of Delhi, Church of North India, Paul Swarup, led a special prayer for the Prime Minister, asking for wisdom and strength. Speaking to India Today, Bishop Swarup emphasised that Christmas is primarily a 'message of peace and joy and reconciliation,' which is crucial in a world facing disturbance and wars. He noted that while the Prime Minister did not speak, he participated in the liturgy to pray for the nation. The report also highlights PM Modi's regular engagement with the Christian community, including his attendance at a dinner at Minister George Kurian's residence in 2024.
