Spread the truth:

A video is viral in which a small gathering of Muslims is shouting slogans against Pakistan and praising India, burning Pakistan’s flag. It’s claimed as recent and from POK.

Viral text – POK में हजारों की संख्या में मुस्लिम समुदाय ने रैली निकाल कर भारत में मिलने की इच्छा जताई, उन्होंने पाकिस्तानी झंडा जलाकर, हिंदुस्तान जिंदाबाद, पाकिस्तान मुर्दाबाद, “सारे जहां से अच्छा हिंदुस्तान हमारा” के नारे लगाए, लेकिन भारत के कुछ लोग पाकिस्तान के गुणगान करते नहीं थकते।

Translation – “Thousands of Muslim took out a rally in POK and claimed their wish is to be a part of India, they burnt flag of Pakistan, shouted India is great, down with Pakistan, Our India is better than entire world but still some people never stop praising Pakistan”

Truth

This video is 3 years old, Sep 2016 from Uttar Pradesh.

In Darool Uloom, Deoband, Many Muslims showed their anger against Pakistan by burning their flag, chanting slogans against Pakistan and in Favor of India after Uri Attack.

Nisar Mehdi, Correspondent of US Daily News Paper, Washintong Post, based in Karachi, Pakistan tweeted the video on 29th Sep 2016.
