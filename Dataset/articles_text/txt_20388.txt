We reverse search the video and found that the video is old and from the year 2020.

The video is actually from the corona times and the two guys in Saffron shawls are the corona warriors. They are going house to house to aware people of the corona and to follow all the corona norms.

The video was shared by the official account of @budaunpolice (Budaun Police).
