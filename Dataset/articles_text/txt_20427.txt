A tweet over Barack Obama is going viral on social media. Roshan Rinaldi posted a tweet supposedly of Former US President Obama where he has raised a question regarding Donald’s Russian birth certificate.

I can't believe he tweeted this. Wow. pic.twitter.com/zXFr50RcFN — Roshan Rinaldi (@Roshan_Rinaldi) April 2, 2022

Many other users have also given a shocked reaction while posting it.

Fact-check

After doing the reverse-image search, we found the creator of this image. Carroll Carpenter an account on Reddit posted the viral Obama tweet in March 2022.

After checking her profile, we found that she wrote about how she makes fake tweets, ads etc. She also claimed that whatever she posts is written by her.

Therefore, the viral image is edited and Barack Obama himself did not made any such tweet on Twitter.
