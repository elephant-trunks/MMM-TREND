AAP MP Sanjay Singh tweets a photo of a woman crying touching a wall with name on martyrs engraved on it claiming she is a widow and blind. People pointer her hand to her Martyr Husband’s name and Sanjay Singh blamed PM Modi for it.

“कारगिल शहीद विधवा जिनकी आँख की रोशनी चली गई ,वार मेमोरीयल पहुँची,लोगों ने इनके पति का नाम उँगलियों से स्पर्श कराया तो आँख से आँसू बहने लगे,पाकिस्तान के साथ युद्ध अभ्यास का फ़ैसला करने वाले @narendramodi जी इनको क्या जवाब देंगे?”

The truth is, she is a sister who is not blind and the photo is on or before Year 2006.

She saw her martyr brother’s name and cried.

“July 20, 2006: A woman breaks down as she reads the name of her brother engraved on the Kargil War Memorial in Drass, 250 kilometers Northeast of Srinagar, summer capital of Indian administered Kashmir Wednesday.
