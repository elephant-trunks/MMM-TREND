A post is becoming very viral on social media. It was claimed that the Election Commission of India (ECI) has announced the re-election of those seats in Uttar Pradesh. Where EVM machines were replaced.

Fact Check:

On verifying the viral claim, we found a fact check of PIB. In which it was said that the claim of replacement of EVMs is fake. Also, re-polling in 142 seats has not been announced by the Election Commission of India (ECI).

Therefore, the claim of re-election in UP is fake.

Conclusion:

The claim is fake.
