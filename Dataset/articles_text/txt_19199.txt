Entrepreneur Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw on August 1 tweeted an old morphed image of a management college which had been sarcastically renamed as 'Rajinikanth Medical College of Engineering for Commerce and Arts' after the Indian actor Rajinikanth.

The viral image has 'can anybody explain what exactly is taught in this college?' written over it. Mazumdar-Shaw tweeted the image with exclamation marks.
