During the course of our investigation, we investigated the images with the help of Google reverse image search and found out that these images were shared by the official verified Twitter handle of deceased Pakistani singer Junaid Jamshed who died in a plane crash in the year 2016.

With this text, we could establish that along with Junaid Jamshed the other person standing with Aamir Khan in this viral picture is Maulana Tariq Jameel who is a reknowned scholar from the Islamic scholar and religious teacher associated with the Tablighi Jamat. Therefore in our investigation, it was found that the claims made on these viral pictures were found to be false. The viral pictures were found to be from the Hajj pilgrimage of Aamir Khan and it does not have any connection with the terrorists.
