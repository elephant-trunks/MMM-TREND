A claim is getting viral on social media where people are claiming that Ratan Tata gave a speech in Symbiosis College, Pune. A superpower speech of Tata concluded that one should never take life seriously, ease out a bit, concentrate on the career and keep a balance of everything in life. Take care of your relationship and mental health too. Users are sharing Tata’s speech as a motivational quote.

Fact Check

After researching we got to know that words in the super speech of Ratan Tata are original of Chetan Bhagat. In short, Ratan Tata didn’t give the viral speech instead Chetan Bhagat gave at Symbiosis, Pune in 2008.

Conclusion- Hence proving that the superpower speech about having balance in life was not given by Ratan Tata rather it was Chetan Bhagat who spoke at Symbiosis, Pune in 2008.
