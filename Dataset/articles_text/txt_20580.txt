Hijab Girl Muskan has come out to be the rising star of the protest happening in Karnataka. Muskan’s act of bravery has got both praise and critics from the people over social media platforms. The fact that Muskan gained overnight popularity and became an inspiration to millions of Hijab girls has emerged as a big claim. A video is going viral on Social media where the users are claiming that Muskan is being honoured by the authorities of Burj Khalifa on 12th February.

Fact check

After seeing the viral claim, we went to the official account of Burj Khalifa on Twitter and thus we didn’t find any such video on the wall. Therefore, we concluded that the video is fake and edited.
