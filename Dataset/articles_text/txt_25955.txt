Former Pakistani Federal Minister for Human Rights, Shireen Mazari of PTI posted a screengrab of a news screen of Pakistani media channel SAMAA TV on her verified Twitter account. On the news plate, it was written in Urdu which can be nearly translated into English that Imran Khan’s win is India’s loss.

Further, along with the picture she wrote, “One more agenda item of US regime change conspiracy will be undone when IK with the nation behind him wins elections. Will end move towards US subservience & signal failure of conspiracy & the local conspirators & will save Pakistan. No wonder US & India fear IK & his Absolutely Not!”

Soon her tweet got viral with more then four thousand retweets and seven thousand likes. Similarly, many other PTI supporters have posted this news.

FactCheck

On DFRAC fact check analysis, our team found that the article of India Today which was referred in the post is old. The article was of 23 July 2018.

Conclusion

Therefore, the claims of the post shared by Shireen Mazari and other PTI supporters is misleading.
