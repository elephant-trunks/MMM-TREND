A video of ex-BJP member Nupur Sharma is getting circulated all over the internet. In the video, Nupur Sharm with a group of people shouting How’s the Josh? High Sir!.

Further, sharing the video a Facebook user named Niki Sidhu wrote in the caption, “Queen After Offending 1 Billion. How is the Josh?”

Similarly, many other users have share this video with the similar claim.

Fact Check

On reverse image search of the different frames of the image we found the original video on the official Twitter handle of Nupur Sharma. She posted this video on 26 Jan, 2019. Hence, this video is not related to the ongoing Prophet remark controversy. Further, she gave the caption, “Celebrating #गणतंत्रदिवस #URITheSurgicalStrike style with old college friends! How’s the Josh?? High Sir!!” Tagging Prime Minister Narendra Modi and actor Vicky Kaushal.

Conclusion

Therefore, from our fact check analysis it is clear that users are sharing an old video of Nupur Sharma with misleading claim.
