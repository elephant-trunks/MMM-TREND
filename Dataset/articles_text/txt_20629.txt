Fact Check

Going through this viral video with the caption ‘BJP again started playing the games to tamper the EVM machines and hashtags like #election2022. we find out that though the video is true but this is not from the recent elections that are on the onset , but is 4 years old news , and nothing like this happened in the year 2022.

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/madhya-pradesh-government-shunts-out-bhind-dm-sp-after-evm-row/articleshow/57968571.cms

Conclusion

This is a misleading news as a four year old video is getting viral under the hashtag of election 2022, and no such tampering of machine is done by BJP in these election.
