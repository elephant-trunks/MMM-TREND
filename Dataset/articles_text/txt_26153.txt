Similarly, this video was forwarded on the WhatsApp multiple times.

Fact Check

In our factcheck analysis, firstly we identified the man in the video as Biswaroop Roy Chowdhury. Moreover, in the video he mention a book

360degree postural medicine. On keyword searching we found that this is the book by Biswaroop Roy Chowdhary.

Then, we found a 20:26 minutes long video on the website of 360degree postural medicine.

Watching the video we got the the viral video is a clip of this video. And, it has no relation with the Tata Memorial. We also search for the same video on the hospital’s website. But, there is no such video available there.
