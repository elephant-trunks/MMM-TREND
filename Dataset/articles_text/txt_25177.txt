Truth

The image is from 2018. Upon doing a Google Reverse Image Search of the image. It led us to a Hindustan Times article published on June 22, 2018.

It was found that the photo was clicked by Waseem Andrabi for Hindustan Times in 2018.

According to the Hindustan Times report, four militants suspected to be running a local module of the Islamic State (IS), a policeman and a civilian were killed in Nowshera village of Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir, on June 22, 2018.

More than 20 civilians were injured in the clashes between the police and the local residents. The image is of the clashes that took place.

Hence, it is established that the image is NOT of people pelting stones at the security forces in Avantipura as claimed in the viral posts. It has to be noted that the image is of 2018 and has no relation to people pelting stones at the security forces after the killing of terrorist Riyaz Naikoo.
