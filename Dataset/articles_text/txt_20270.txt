After the murder of a famous Punjab singer and Congress leader Sidhu Moosewala, social media has been flooded with pictures and videos of him in reactionary sorrow and anger. Similarly, a video that is going viral from this content is being claimed by users that this is the last interview of Sidhu Moosewala.

A user named Salman Khan shared a video with the caption, “Sidhu moose wala last interview.”In which he answers questions from program host Sonam Bajwa about his life and career. In the end, he also enacts with Sonam in a scene.

Fact Check:

We did a reverse image search on the internet to find some frames of this video of Sidhu Moosewala going viral on social media. We found that this video was uploaded on YouTube on June 28, 2021, as the 13th episode of the channel Zee Punjabi’s show “Dil Diyan Gallan”.

At the same time, the video of this entire interview has been uploaded on the OTT platform “ZEE5” on 06 March 2021.

An article related to Sidhu Moose Wala has been published on a website called Newsable, in which the video of this interview has also been mentioned.

Conclusion:

This fact check by DFRAC proves that the video clip of the interview which is going viral on social media is old, hence users are sharing it with a misleading claim.
