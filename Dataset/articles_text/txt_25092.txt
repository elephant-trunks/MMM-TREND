Truth

The photo used to show current situation of children in India is 12 years old, from Bangladesh

The photo has been taken by a Bangladeshi award-winning photojournalist GMB Akash. He took this photo in Jan 2008, of child labor in Brick Factory in Fatullah near Dhaka, Bangladesh.

He posted his own photo in 2010 on his verified Facebook page

“G.M.B. Akash

Fatullah, BANGLADESH

Children at a brick factory in Fatullah. For each thousand bricks they carry, they earn the equivalent of 0.9 USD. 17.5 percent of all children aged between 5-15 are engaged in economic activities. The average child labourer earns between 400 to 700 taka (1 USD = 70 taka) per month, while an adult worker earns up to 5,000 taka per month.”
