Finland has been named the world’s happiest country for the fifth year running, and Afghanistan is ranked as the unhappiest, closely followed by Lebanon.

Meanwhile, social media is covered with the claims that Pakistan is ranked 67 whereas India is ranked 120 in the world happiness report.
