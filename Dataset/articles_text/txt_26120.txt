IAS couple Sanjeev Khirwar and wife Rinku Dugga had recently faced transfers for taking a dog walk at the Tyagaraja Stadium in the national capital. Meanwhile, a video is going viral on social media, In which a woman can be seen getting angry.

Sharing the video, the Facebook user wrote that after receiving the IAS Madam Transfer Order. In the 10-second clip, the woman can be heard saying in Urdu, “What kind of rule is this?” Whose order was it? Heembarrassed us. We’re all ashamed. I cried at night. I’m crying..

Fact Check:

To check the above claim, we did a reverse search of the keyframe of the video. During this time, we got a similar video from Pakistan on YouTube. In the video, the woman is showing her displeasure towards the Imran government. The woman is a fan of Maryam Nawaz Sharif.

Conclusion:

Hence, the claim of the viral video being of an IAS couple from Delhi is fake.
