Fact Check:

DFRAC reverse searched the video using specific key frames. We found the news of the same incident on the news portal of NDTV.

The headline states, “On Camera, Hyderabad Teen Flung Into Air After Being Hit By Car.” news was of the date 7th july 2022.

The Times of India too covered the same news.

In the report, it is written that a 19-year-old man, who also lives in the same locality and was learning to drive when this happened.

Conclusion:

DFRAC found that there was no Hindu Muslim angle to the viral video, neither it was done on purpose. It was clearly an accident.
