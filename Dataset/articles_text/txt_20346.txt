An image of people offering Nawaz is going viral on the internet. People are claiming that the picture is of outside Bandra Railway Station. Further, along with the picture a social media user @vinit_pandit243 wrote in the caption, “Bandra Railway Station (west) in Mumbai But हनुमान चालीसा not allowed.”

Similarly another user @ShwetankSeeks wrote, “This is outside Bandra Railway station (can’t miss the famous stone entry gate). Image available in public domain. Can do a reverse lookup through Google Lens.“

The picture is getting viral with many derogatory comments

Fact Check

On our analysis we found that this is an old picture. We found this picture on gettyimages.

The creation date of the image is 17 August, 2012.

Moreover, the image is also available at the page of The Atlantic.

Hence, this is an old image. But, people are sharing it in a misleading way.
