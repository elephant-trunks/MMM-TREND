A picture is getting viral on social media sites on social media site. In the picture, the President of the All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen Asaduddin Owaisi can be seen and on the top of the picture there is written in capital letters “EFFECTIVE LEADERS”

Many users are praising Owaisi while sharing the same picture on social media sites.

A user on Twitter writes, “#AIMIM Chief Barrister @asadowaisi Has Been Spotted In Australian University’s Professional Presentation As a Top Best Effective Leaders. #proud #merasadar #meraguroor.”

Another user writes, “Proud movement for indian parliment that one of a member has been selected as an effective leader ! V proud of u sir.”

Proud movement for indian parliment that one of a member has been selected as a effective leader ! V proud of u sir https://t.co/QG7QRAg5bs — Syed Ifteqar Ahmed (@SyedIfteqarAhm2) January 17, 2020

Fact Check:

To investigate the above claim DFRAC desk searched on Google using specific keywords and found no news reports related to this in the mainstream media channels and sites.

To continue the search DFRAC desk looked for the most effective leaders of the world and again found no mention of Owaisi in it.

Conclusion:

The picture is fake as there is no mention of Owaisi in any valid searches done by the desk.
