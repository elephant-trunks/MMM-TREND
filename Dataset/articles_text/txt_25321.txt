Spread the truth:

Congress MP Digvijay Singh tweeted a photo of many ambulance lying idle, rotting somewhere. He tweeted this as Yogi Adityanath’s failure claiming these ambulances are in Uttar Pradesh.

He tweeted –

“ये उत्तर प्रदेश की हालत क्या कर दी योगी जी ने, अखिलेश यादव जी द्वारा चलवाई गई 108,102 एम्बूलेंस खड़ी जंग खा रही है और जनता अपने बीमार परिजनों को ठेलो पर अस्पताल ले जा रही है॥ #वाह_योगी_जी_वाह# #बेशरमी_की_भी_हद_होती_है# रजत यादव”

Translation – “What has CM Yogi Adityanath has done to Uttar Pradesh, the ambulance service 108, 102 started by Akhilesh Yadav is rotting while people are taking their sick relatives to hospital on handcarts. Great Yogi Ji, there has to be a limit of shamelessness

ये उत्तर प्रदेश की हालत क्या कर दी योगी जी ने, अखिलेश यादव जी द्वारा चलवाई गई 108,102 एम्बूलेंस खड़ी जंग खा रही है और जनता अपने बीमार परिजनों को ठेलो पर अस्पताल ले जा रही है॥

Truth –

1. You can see at the top of Ambulances something is written in a South Indian Language.

2. You can clearly see “Government of Andhra Pradesh” is written on it, not Uttar Pradesh.

This photo was posted by a south Indian news website “Sakshi.com” on 22nd September as one of the photos from the week. It was taken by from Vijayawada. They posted the photo under their Best Weekend photos, 38th Week

Under the image it read “ఆపత్కాల వరదాయిని 108 అటకెక్కింది..! (ఫొటో: కిశోర్‌, విజయవాడ)”

Translated – “The 108 Emergency Response Service went up the attic (Photo: Kishore, Vijayawada)”.
