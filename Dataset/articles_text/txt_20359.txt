In the month of Ramzan, a video is getting viral on the internet. In the video, it seems that a group of people is fighting in the mosque.

People are sharing the video with sarcastic comments on Twitter.

A user writes, “After being hungry all day in Ramadan, peacemakers calmly urging each other to eat in the evening.”

https://twitter.com/ShrishtySays/status/1519261499679674368?s=20&t=4Dj2SVxvi-4_ar0C27u9EA

Fact Check:

To know the truth behind the video DFRAC reverse searched the video using certain keywords.

We found that this video is not from any Indian mosque and was a fight in Saudi Arabia. Also, the video is from the year 2019.

We got the same news covered by a newspaper Morocco News World.

The same was twitted by a user back then criticizing people those were indulged in fight.

“For revival, no shyness, no respect for the mosque, and no appreciation for the holy month such people should be held accountable Allah is my suffice, and the best deputy.” Reads the Caption.
