A viral clip from Mexico showing an old man being administered a vaccine using an empty syringe has been shared on social media with false claims.

The video is viral amid the spike in COVID-19 cases in the deadly second wave of the pandemic. According to Ministry of Health's data, India has vaccinated total 14,09,16,417 people. In April, India approved Russia's Sputnik V COVID-19 vaccine, the country is so far administering two vaccines, one developed by Astra Zeneca and Oxford University and the other by Hyderabad based firm by Bharat Biotech.

The 30 second viral clip shows a closer look of the health worker administering the vaccine with an empty syringe to an elderly man.
