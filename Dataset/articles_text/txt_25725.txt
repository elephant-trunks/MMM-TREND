The video of a temple, decorated with currency notes, is being shared on social media with a claim that it’s of Tirupati Balaji Temple in Andhra Pradesh.

Facebook users are sharing the video with the caption: “तिरुपति बालाजी मंदिर आंध्र प्रदेश में फूलों की जगह भारतीय नोटों से हुई सजावट की अनोखी कारीगरी की झलक.” (English translation: A glimpse of unique decoration with Indian notes instead of flowers in Tirupati Balaji temple of Andhra Pradesh)

Here’s the link to the above post.

FACT CHECK

NewsMobile fact-checked the post, and found it to be misleading.

We ran a keyword search on YouTube and found a similar video, uploaded on October 11, 2021, with the title: “Sri Kanyaka Parameswari temple decorated with currency notes| Nellore | Dussehra 2021 | SumanTv.”

Similar videos were uploaded by other channels on YouTube in October 2021. The videos can be seen here and here.
