Fact check:

To know the reality of the viral screengrab we first check the verified Twitter handle of Elon Musk. And, we got to know that Elon Musk is still active on Twitter.

Furthermore, amidst the rumors, we also got one of his tweets in which he wrote, “Oh the irony lol.”

Oh the irony lol — Elon Musk (@elonmusk) July 12, 2022

The difference between suspended account And Active account

The original account of Elon Musk of @elonmusk and the account that got suspended used the letter I(capital eye) instead of the letter L(l).

Conclusion :

Therefore, from this fact check it is clear that That Elon Musk’s Twitter account has not got suspended. The account that was suspend is fake. Hence, the claim of social media users are false.
