A video is allegedly shared on the internet where it can be seen a man is brutally assaulting women.

The video is shared with the claim that the man seen in the video is a Muslim named Abdul whereas the woman who is facing the assault is a Hindu.

The incident is said to catch fuel when the Hindu family told Abdul not to throw garbage in front of their home.

The video is all over Facebook.

Fact Check:

We at DFRAC reverse searched the video and found that the news was found on several local news channels on 22nd January 2022. It was then found the it was not a fight of a Muslim against Hindu, but only between two Muslim families.

An official YouTube channel ZMDM News shared same video.

https://youtu.be/6zUDM-7Og_s

Conclusion:

The video is getting viral with a misleading claim of communal roits, but in actuality it was between the same communities.

Claim Review: Muslim man assaulting a Hindu woman in Mirzapur.

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact Check: misleading
