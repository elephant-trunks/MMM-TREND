A video of Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan coming out from a women’s toilet is going viral on social media. In the viral video, Shivraj is coming out of a toilet. On the walls of that toilet, it was written that Women’s toilet. After this, he is greeting the people by shaking hands with them.

A user named Sachin Dubey wrote on Facebook – “ Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan Ji coming out of the women’s toilet.

Fact Check:

On reverse-searching some of the frames of this viral video, we found a video uploaded on YouTube. This video was uploaded on a YouTube channel named ‘ Global Herald News ‘ on 19th April 2019. The titled of the video is, “ Khargone- Shivraj Singh Chouhan entered the women’s toilet . Shivraj Singh Chauhan enters women’s toilet” .

This is the same video that is being shared by social media users.

Conclusion:

Therefore, our fact check proves that the viral video is 3 years old. Hence, the claim of social media users is misleading.

Claim- CM Shivraj coming out of the women’s toilet

Claimant- Sachin Dubey and other Social media users
