Fact Check

Through the research on various sites, it was found out that the picture getting viral is of BJP Leaders Rahul Gandhi and Priyanka Gandhi are basically photoshopped . This is taken from the picture of the Manifesto of congress for the UP elections, Congress is claiming to provide 20 lakhs jobs , out of which 8 lakh posts will be for women. A Hashtag is also provided for the manifesto under #कांग्रेस_का_भर्ती_विधान to bring back the smiles on the faces of youth of UP.

Conclusion

On researching it was found out that the picture getting viral is of manifesto of Congress party and, no book named “aaege toh Yogi hi” has been launched by congress leaders Priyanka Gandhi and Rahul Gandhi.
