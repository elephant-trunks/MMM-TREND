A video was shared by Prashant Umrao, a Standing Counsel in Supreme Court of India for the State of Goa and a Spokesperson for BJP (Bharatiya Janta Party) official account in which he was humiliating SP (Samajwadi Party) in his caption stating, “सपा के जुलूस में लहराये सैकड़ो अवैध तमंचे। सपा के पूर्व कैबिनेट मंत्री व विधानसभा प्रत्याशी शाहिद मंजूर के बेटे नवाज़िश के स्वागत में हुई फायरिंग। जुलूस में हथियारों के साथ डांस और स्वागत में फायरिंग का वीडियो वायरल किठौर विधानसभा का है वायरल वीडियो। तमंचवादी समाजवादी पार्टी|”

This translates into, “Hundreds of illegal firearms were waved in the SP procession. Firing took place at the reception of Nawazish, son of former SP cabinet minister and assembly candidate Shahid Manzoor. Video of dance with weapons in procession and firing at reception goes viral Viral video of Kithor assembly.”

Fact Check:

Through our analysis, we got that this video is not from recent times as claimed by Prashant Umrao. It was of the year 2016. It was also found out that the whole issue is of Meerut.

It was also cleared by UP Police fact check official account where it was clearly stated that by spreading such fake news , one shall not mislead people.
