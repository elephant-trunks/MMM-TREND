Fact Check

Using the InVID tool and applying reverse image search on different keyframes of the viral video, the DFRAC team found it on YouTube on Sansad TV. The title of the video is, “President Kovind’s departure from the Central Hall of Parliament I Farewell function.”

In the 8:57 minutes long video, at 1.09 minutes we can the clip that has gone viral. Further, after delivering his farewell speech, Kovind was greeting the attendees with folded hands. In the video, it can be seen that Kovind has greeted Modi. But, the social media users have cropped that part from the viral video and have shared the video with misleading claims.

Conclusion

Therefore, from our fact check it is clear that social media users are sharing the viral video with the misleading claim.
