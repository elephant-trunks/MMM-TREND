A video clip showing miraculous escape for a Manipur student has gone viral on the social media. The video shows a student falling under a moving oil tanker.

He, however, somehow manages to escape from being crushed under the wheels of the tanker. A close look at the video shows he was hit by a cane by a cop who was trying to pacify a mob of protesting students in the capital city of Imphal on Monday.

The critically injured student was rushed to a hospital on a mini truck where he is stated to be stable. The Manipur University Students Union (MUSU) has been protesting for over a month demanding the removal of the Vice-Chancellor (V-C). The University has been shut since.

India Today Fact Check Team investigated the video and came up with the details of the entire episode:



The video was shot on July 16 by a passerby. The group of students, led by Democratic Students Alliance of Manipur (DESAM), were staging a protest in front Manipur Pradesh BJP headquarters on Nityai Path Chuthek in Imphal. The students were confronted by the Manipur Police and during which, a sub-inspector hit Oinam Shital, General Secretary of DESAM. Oinam fell on the road and was nearly run over by the oil tanker. Oinam was rushed to a private nursing home where he was operated for multiple fractures on his left shoulder, collar bone and arm. Manipur Chief Minister N Biren Singh visited the hospital on Thursday to check on Oinam's health and has assured action against the accused cop.

Manipur University has been shut for 50 days. The University teachers and staff have also joined the student's agitation against the V-C.
