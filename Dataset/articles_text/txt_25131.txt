However, the building in question that can be seen in the video is not the White House.

A report filed by NBC4i, a Columbus based news website stated that “Protest in downtown Columbus broken up after demonstrators breach Ohio Statehouse”. A video from the protest as posted under this article can be viewed here. Screenshots of the same is as follows:

A Google Reverse Search Image showed that the building shown in the video closely represents the Ohio Statehouse.
