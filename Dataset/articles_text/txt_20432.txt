A video is getting viral on the internet of a drunk man. Users are claiming that he is Punjab’s chief Minister Bhagwant Mann. Further sharing the video Sandeep Goswami, wrote the caption, “In the joy of victory, flowers have come out tight, the next Chief Minister of Punjab.”

Similarly, many other users have shared this image with sarcastic comments.

Fact Check

On our fact check analysis we found the same video on the profile of @TajinderBagga. But the video was shared on March 4, 2017. Further, the caption of the video is, “I don’t drink, I haven’t drink. I have been given a drink, yes, I have been given a drink.”

Maine Pi nahi hai, Maine Pi nahi hai. Mujhe pila di gai hai, hanji mujhe pila di gai hai pic.twitter.com/qwcQNjYpT7 — Tajinder Bagga (Modi Ka Parivar) (@TajinderBagga) March 4, 2017

Hence, seeing the post it is clear that the viral video is from 2017. Bhagwant Mann is Punjab’s newly elected Chief Minister. In the past also DFRAC has already covered many fake or misleading claims related to Bhagwant Mann.

Conclusion

Therefore, users are sharing old videos with misleading claims about Punjab’s Chief Minister Bhagwant Mann.
