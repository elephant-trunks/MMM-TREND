An image is getting viral on the internet with the claims that Police arrested 25 people in Allahabad university who were making bombs. It is written in the image that Allahabad University has become the new center of terrorists. Twenty-five students were caught while making the bomb. And, fifty-eight rooms were sealed. But, the media is silent as no one among them is Muslim.

Fact Check

On reverse image search we found this image on a report of mandsaursandeshnewspaper. In the report, it was stated that police have arrested 15 men and 8 women for running a sex racket.

Moreover, on keyword research, we found several media reports of 2019 where an incident of Police raiding the hostel of Allahabad University was reported. And, raw materials for making a bomb and crude bombs were also found.

Conclusion

The information given in the viral picture is old and the image used is not related to the incidence.
