An image of Masjid is going viral on the internet. Sharing the image people are claiming that the image is of the same Masjid where the riots took place Further, along with the picture @realsanatanis wrote, “Karoli Rajasthan The mosque from which the stones were thrown…!

Climbing on the same mosque, the Banjara Dal waved saffron…”

Similarly, many other users shared this image linking with the Karauli Rajasthan incident.

Fact Check

On image reverse search we found the same image on alamy.com where it is mentioned that the viral image was taken on 26th October 2011.

Moreover, we also found the same picture on stock adobe.com and bigstockphoto.

Conclusion

Therefore, it is clear that it is an old image. But, users are misleadingly linking with the recent Karauli riots.
