Fact Check

Searching with some keywords and doing reverse image searches, we got to know that the viral picture of Pinarayi Vijayan and Yusuff Ali is from December, 2021. When the LuLu Mall was inaugurated in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. India Today Malayalam also published a report on the same.

Moreover, on 16th of December 2021, Pinarayi Vijayan posted a tweet tagging Thani Al Zeyoudi (UAE Minister of State for Foreign Trade) which can be seen below.

The UAE has promised to open a state-of-the-art food park in Kerala. @ThaniAlZeyoudi, the UAE Minister of State for Foreign Trade, has said the details would be discussed with the technical team. Talking about the second picture, which was taken in the inauguration of LuLu Mall in Lucknow on 10th of July. Yusuff Ali is seen sitting beside the CM of UP, Yogi Adityanath, while driving the buggy. The pictures are covered in the reports of onmanorama, jagran

Conclusion

By our fact check it is clear that the post shared in the social media is misleading. The man driving the buggy for the Kerala CM is not a chauffeur but a minister of UAE.
