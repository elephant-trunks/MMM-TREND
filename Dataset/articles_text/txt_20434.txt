The same kind of caption and post were posted on Facebook by many users.

Fact Check:

To know the authenticity of this video we reverse search the Image and used certain key words.

We found the same video on YouTube channel of Times Express from the year 2020. The man giving the speech is a well-known lawyer Bhanu Pratap Singh who gave the speech to the crowd sitting in the protest of NRC-CAA after the verdict of the Supreme Court.

We too check the official site of Himalaya to know the name of the owner of the company and found his name to be Mr. M Manal.

Conclusion:

Hence, the video is getting viral with a misleading claim.

Claim Review: the owner of Himalaya Company provoking people to boycott Reliance Jio.

Claimed by: Social media users.

Fact check: misleading
