The viral photograph is photoshopped, and also the hoarding doesnтАЩt belong to Wayanad, Kerala. The hoarding is from the Rangareddy district of Telangana and it is three years old.

We also discovered that a Telugu website named Telugu Rajyam had also debunked the viral claim in August 2018. Fact-checking news websites such as Alt News and Boom Live had also debunked the viral claim back in April 2019. Therefore, we conclude that the viral image is photoshopped and is just an attempt to mock Rahul Gandhi.
