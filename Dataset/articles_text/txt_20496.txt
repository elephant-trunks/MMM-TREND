The wave of PM Narendra Modi-led BJP government was seen in the assembly elections of four states this year. Consequently, Modi supporters were enthusiastically hailed for the BJP and Modi. Internet got flooded with many posts related to this grand victory. Meanwhile, many people shared the picture of the New York Stock Exchange with a digital scroll that showed the news of the BJP’s win “in India’s biggest state.” sharing the image @MODIfyingINDIA2014 wrote in the caption, “Just ZOOM and see at New York Stock exchange what is displayed in the digital display.”

Similarly, many other users shared this image on their social media handles.

Fact Check

After the reverse image search,a we found that this is an old image. Moreover, the image is not of NSE. Above all, this image got viral in 2017 also.

Conclusion

Hence, from our Fact check analysis it is clear that this image is fake. It has no relation with the BJP victory in assembly elections this year.
