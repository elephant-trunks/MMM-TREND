A video recently has got viral where it is shown some people showing black flags to Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh Yogi Adityanath’s convoy. People are claiming that this is a recently shot video and sharing it on social media sites using “*Breaking News*” in their tweet.

Fact Check

After performing fact check analysis by our team we got to know that this video is false and misleading. This footage is not recently captured. It is from the year 2017. NDTV and bhaskar has also covered this story back in 2017.

“Lucknow University students were arrested for protesting against the spending on a function attended by UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath” read the sub-heading of NDTV report.

Therefore, we can clearly say that this video is false and misleading and is shared with the false claim.
