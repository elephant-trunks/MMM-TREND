A photo with a face mask that has the name and logo of Delhi Public School (DPS) is shared on social media claiming that DPS is selling them to its students at a cost of Rs 350/- per mask. But as the message went viral, managements of various branches of DPS across the country have clarified through social media posts that their School is not selling those masks. These posts can be seen here, here and here. Mansur Ali, board member of DPS has also sent messages to parents of its students that the masks in the photo are not associated with DPS.
