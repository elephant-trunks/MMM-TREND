A video is getting viral on the social media platforms of an elderly man playing cricket. Users are claiming that the man in the video is PM Narendra Modi. Moreover, sharing the video aryan____jaiswal____wrote, “@Narendra Modi Playing Cricket.”

Similarly, many users have shared this video on their handles.

Fact Check

Using InVID tool and google image search, we found that the man in the video is not PM Narendra Modi but he is Yograj Singh, Father of famous cricketer Yuvraj Singh. We found a post on his Facebook account wearing the same outfit as was wore by the man in the viral video.
