After the breakdown of Karauli violence, social media was filled with fake, misleading news and hatred all around. Another video has got viral on social media regarding the Rajasthan election. Users are posting a small video where a group of people are taking an oath to not casting vote in favour of congress.

टोनी गौरव त्यागी posted the viral video and claimed that the people of Rajasthan are boycotting the governance of Congress in the state.

The video is getting viral in April 2022. संघर्ष सत्य के लिए, also claimed that Hindus of Rajasthan are taking an oath to boycott the Congress.

Fact check

After doing the cross-check, we found that the video is a year old.

राजस्थान बेरोजगार छात्रसंघ posted the video on 17 Nov 2021 on YouTube claiming that Upen Yadav gave a message regarding the unemployment in the state to the public gathered at Ramleela Maidan.

A Rajasthani news portal known as National Dunia also covered the news of the unemployed taking oath for boycotting Congress in Zilla Parishad Panchayat Samiti elections and assembly by-elections.

Therefore, the news is wrongly spread as it is a year old.

Conclusion: The claim is misleading

Claim review: Rajasthan’s people take the oath for boycotting Congress due to unemployment in the state

Claim by: संघर्ष सत्य के लिए

Fact check: misleading
