A picture of famous Indian actor Sanjay Dutt is getting viral on social media sites where he can be seen wearing the holy cap of the Muslims with lots of food kept in front of him.

The image of the actor is getting viral with the claim that Sanjay Dutt is breaking the fast after accepting Islam. It is written in Urdu over the image.

Conclusion:

DFRAC desk found the image of actor Sanjay Dutt is photoshopped and is from the year 2019.
