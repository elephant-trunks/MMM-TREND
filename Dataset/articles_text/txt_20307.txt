A video is going viral on social media sites. Further, users are sharing the video with the claim that a Muslim girl was brutally beaten up by her brothers for dating a non-Muslim boy. Meanwhile, a Twitter user named Tin Man posted the English caption “ Bro beats girl for wanting to date a non-Muslim in America. ,

Fact Check :

After keyword searching, we found that the video was covered by a Russian website called Life and Bloknot with the headline “In Krasnodar, relatives filmed how a young gypsy woman who wanted to run away to a guy was severely beaten.”

Also, on reverse image search, we found the same video on , the social media account of REBORN posted on 06 July 2021. And, the caption of the post reads, “In Krasnodar, they beat and humiliated a young gypsy woman who wanted to escape to the guy in Ukraine. They beat their own relatives. According to some reports, she alone kept unemployed father and brothers. And then they ask why everyone hates them.”

Conclusion:

Hence, our Fact check proves that users are sharing the viral video with false claim. The video is of Russia not of America.And, the lover of the girl also belongs to Muslim community.

Claim: Muslim Guy beats her sister for dating a non-Muslim in America.

Claimed By: Social media users

Fact Check: Misleading
