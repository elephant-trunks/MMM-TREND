FAKE NEWS

Pioneer TV has spread out fake news about the lockdown in Telangana.

#3rdWave Begins in India…#lockdown In Telangana says the Telugu account.

Fact check

The news is false. Telangana government has not made any such decisions or statements. They have declared holidays in all the educational institutions across the state on the occasion of Makar Sankranti from 8th January to 16th January and after witnessing a spike in covid-19 cases. The cases are reported from Rangareddy and Medchal-Malkajgiri districts. The Telangana government has also made clear by not imposing lockdown and night curfew in the upcoming days in the report by Indian Express.

{Indian Express article: https://indianexpress.com/article/cities/hyderabad/telangana-covid-cases-schools-closed-sero-survey-lockdown-news-7705118/}

Conclusion

Before believing in any widely spread picture or a tweet, kindly check the authenticity of the news by renowned sources. As not every tweet made on Twitter is news.
