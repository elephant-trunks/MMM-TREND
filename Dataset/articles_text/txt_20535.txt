A screenshot of Bollywood actress Sara Ali Khan’s tweet regarding the ongoing hijab controversy in Karnataka is going viral. In which it was said that Muslim girls wear hijab only in Muslim areas because they are sexually harassed there.

Fact Check:

To check the above screenshot, we first checked Sara Ali Khan’s Twitter account. During this we found that the verified Twitter account of Sara Ali Khan is in the name of @SaraAliKhan. Whereas the username @WhoSaraAli has been given in the screenshot.

When this account was investigated, it was found that there is no such account on Twitter. At the same time, we did not find any such tweet on Sara Ali Khan’s verified Twitter account @SaraAliKhan.

Conclusion:

So it is clear that the screenshot of the tweet going viral in the name of Sara Ali Khan is fake.

Claim Review: Sara Ali Khan tweeted about the Hijab controversy.

Claimed by: @WhoSaraAli

Fact Check: fake
