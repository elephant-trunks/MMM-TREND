Fact Check

With the research on various platforms it was found out that Prime Minister Narendra Modi has made no such promises under TUDA Township project, but under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojna . It is a move that will benefit about 1.47 lakh people of Tripura living in ‘kuccha’ houses to construct ‘pucca’ dwelling units, Prime Minister Narendra Modi transferred the fund on 14th November 2021, at about 1p.m and directly transferred Rs 700 crore to their accounts for the purpose.

CONCLUSION

It was seen that the information about TUDA township project and Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojna was highly misinterpreted with each other, hence making this picture untrue.
