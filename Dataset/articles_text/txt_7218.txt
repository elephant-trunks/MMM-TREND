A post is being shared on social media with a claim that the Government of India providing 1.5 Lakh rupees per COVID-19 patient to every municipality. Let’s verify the claim made in the post.

The archived version of the post can be found here.

Claim: Government of India providing Rupees 1.5 Lakh per COVID-19 patient to every municipality. Fact: Press Information Bureau (PIB) through a tweet clarified that no such announcement was made by Government of India. Hence the claim made in the post is FALSE.

On google search using the keywords ‘Government of India providing 1.5 lakh rupees to every municipality for each COVID-19 patient’, we did not find any information about the existence of such scheme by the central government. But we found a clarification tweet posted by Press Information Bureau (PIB) on the same subject. PIB in their tweet clarified that no such announcement of providing 1.5 lakh rupees per COVID-19 patient to every municipality was made by Government Of India.

Claim: A message circulating on #WhatsApp claims that Central Government is providing Rs 1.5 lakh to every Municipality for each #COVID19 patient. #PIBFactCheck: The claim is #Fake. No such announcement has been made by Government. pic.twitter.com/Ntr137aIUY — PIB Fact Check (@PIBFactCheck) August 25, 2020

To sum it up, there is no announcement of providing 1.5 Lakh rupees per COVID-19 patient to every municipality from Government of India.
