A video of Nitin Gadkari is getting viral on social media sites.In the video during the interview Nitin can be heard criticising the PM.

It is written on the video, for the first time in a press conference Gadkari openly protested against the PM. Why did Gadkari have to give a suggestion to Narendra Modi?

Social media users are sharing this video with the claim that Nitin is criticising Modi government.

A user on Twitter writes, “@yadavakhilesh BJP President Shiri Nitin Gadkari is raising many questions today PM modi should listen.Because everyone is coming to know that the country is being destroyed.”

Many users shared the video with different captions, but similar claim.

Fact Check:-

To investigate the viral claim, DFRAC desk for used specific keyframe from Google.

After searching, the DFRAC desk did a Google reverse image, and found this video on the YouTube channel of Bharatiya Janata Party uploaded on 16 Aug 2011.

At that time Nitin Gadkari was national president of BJP and there was a Congress government so, Nitin Gadkari suggested the then Prime Minister Manmohan Singh to look at the statement given by Anna Hazare.

Conclusion:

As the video was upload in the year 2011, so it is clear that the video is 10 years old and is getting viral with a misleading claim.

Claim Review: Nitin Gadkari criticising PM Modi.

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact Check: Misleading
