Truth

This is a two year old video, it was viral then too. In Sep 2017, Kolkata Police clarified to SMHoaxSlayer that the people beating the man are family members of a girl whom the man molested. This has nothing to to with Religion or Politics.

In reply to SMHoaxSlayer’s query, Kolkata Police Tweeted: “This is a wrong and mischievous post. This priest was manhandled by the family members of the victim girl where he allegedly molested her. FIR was registered vide no.P3-205 dated 31.08. 17,u/s 354/509 IPC and counter FIR was registered against people who assaulted the priest. vide no P3 Case no 206dated 31.08. 17 u/s 34/323/341IPC. Legal action is being initiated against all the people spreading communal hatred.”

SMHoaxSlayer queried Kolkatta police quoting the tweet in the question
