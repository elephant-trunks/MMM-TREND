Fact Check

We did a reverse image search to fact-check the photo going viral. During this, we found the similar photo on the website of Wikimedia Commons. According to the description of this photo, it was published on 28 July 2008. This photo is of a city bus in Somerset, England. This photo was captured by a photographer named Adrian Pingstone.

Conclusion

Our fact check is proving that BabasahebтАЩs photo on the bus has been edited. His edited photo is being shared by social media users with a misleading claim.
