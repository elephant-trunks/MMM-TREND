Every other day social media is filled with some news related to Rahul Gandhi. This time a social media user has shared a picture with the claim that Rajkumar of Gandhi’s family is on a foreign trip.

Further, he gave a caption to the picture, “India’s economy is failing while the Rajkumar of Gandhi family is enjoying abroad. #Satire.”

Fact Check

People may troll Rahul Gandhi on this image. But, in reality, the man in the picture with Dakota Johnson is not Rahul Gandhi. He is Dakota’s co-actor in the Fifty shades of grey movie series, Jamie Dornan. Moreover, we found the same picture on the website of Elle in an article of 7th September 2021.

Further, it’s not the first time Rahul Gandhi’s Photo has gone viral with Dakota Johnson. Earlier also, social media users picked a picture of Jamie Dornan carrying Dakota Johnson in arms for their movie. And, trolled Rahul Gandhi.

Conclusion

There may be a slight resemblance between the faces of Rahul Gandhi and the actor Jamie Dornan. But the viral picture is not of Rahul Gandhi. Therefore, some social media users are sharing the picture with fake claims.
