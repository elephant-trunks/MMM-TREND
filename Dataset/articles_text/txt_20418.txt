A video is getting viral on the internet with people taking their hands via the bottom of their thighs and holding their ears in a squat position. They are moving slowly and some policemen are monitoring them. By sharing the video users are claiming that they got punishment for reciting their prayers on the road. Further, along with the video @sbsaraswati wrote in the caption, “Punishment for offering Namaz on the road… This is UP Yogi Ji’s poultry farm.”

https://twitter.com/sbsaraswati/status/1512047562190065665

Similarly, many other users have shared this videos with a lot of mocking comments.

https://twitter.com/Yogisaransh_1/status/1512010907181715460

Fact Check

On taking a screenshot of this video and doing a reverse image search on it we found that this video is from Mansehra, Pakistan. We found a video on YouTube on the channel named Urdu Updates. The video was shared on YouTube on March 29, 2020, with the caption, “How Mansehra police punish who violate lockdown, video is viral now. ”

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eHwiSLVnJ1s

Moreover, we also found a report of geo.tv.

Therefore, the claim with the viral video is misleading.
