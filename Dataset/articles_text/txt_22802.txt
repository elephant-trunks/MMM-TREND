Doctors in Kolkata have been on protest over the past few days after two of their colleagues were beaten up by relatives of a patient who died during treatment. Amid the stand-off, many on social media claimed that one of the injured doctors has passed away.

Junior doctor Paribaha Mukhopadhyay and Yash Tekwani are the victims who got beaten up at the NRS medical college, Kolkata and it is claimed that Paribaha Mukhopadhyay has passed away.
