A video is being spread like wildfire on Facebook with the claim that the Indian Army is torturing Kashmiri women. In the video, some men in uniform are seen assaulting women in public. An emotional music plays in the background.

Facebook user Shamim Hossain posted the video on September 8. Since then, it has been viewed more than 4,35,000 times and shared over 38,000 times.

India Today Anti Fake News War Room (AFWA) has found that the video is not from India but Pakistan. The archived version of the post can be seen here.

A close look at the video shows the men in uniform wearing badges displaying the Pakistan flag. This independently clarifies that the video is from Pakistan.

It can also be established that the uniform of the cops seen in the viral video is of Pakistan's police force.

With the help of related keywords, we found the viral video on YouTube too. The title of the YouTube video also says that the incident took place in Pakistan's Multan, where police beat up women outside a court.

Many users in the comment section also pointed out that the claim with the video is false and it is from Pakistan.

We scanned the Internet thoroughly but couldn't find any credible news reports regarding the video. However, from the above pieces of evidence, it is clear that the video is from Pakistan.
