After Aam Adami Party has declared that they intend to contest for all the 68 seats in the Himachal Pradesh Election 2022. A roar of news and claims have been witnessed for the Himachal Pradesh election2022.

Nighat Abbass is a verified account and a spokesperson for BJP who tweeted while inserting a picture of a billboard carrying Arvind Kejriwal saying ladies of Himachal will get 1000rs per month.

Many other users posted the same picture claiming it is a new election campaign of Kejriwal to win the election.

https://twitter.com/doctorrichabjp/status/1512327825419767815?s=20&t=w1hEt85MymofFtJ9T0fqPw

Fact check

After doing the reverse image search, we found that the image is from the year 2015. When Kejriwal won the election and was about to take oath as per India Today.

After seeing the picture, it is clear that the picture is edited and has a misleading claim.

Conclusion: the claim is misleading

Claim review: Arvind Kejriwal promised to give 1000rs to the ladies of Himachal Pradesh

Claim by: Nighat Abbass

Fact check: misleading
