A big claim was made on social media about former Rajasthan Chief Minister Vasundhara Raje Scindia. In which it was said that VasundharaRaje left BJP to join Congress.

A user on Facebook shared the video and said that “Vasundhara left BJP will join Congress”.

Fact check

To check the above claim, we first checked VasundharaRaje’s social media handles – Twitter, Facebook, Instagram. But we did not get any information about this. Then we saw the media section of the official website of VasundharaRaje. But there too no information was found about this.

However, we did get a tweet from Navbharat Times. In which he accused the Gehlot government of Congress of misuse of power in the RajyaSabha elections.

Conclusion:

Therefore, the claim of VasundharaRaje leaving the BJP and joining Congress is fake.

Claim Review: VasundharaRaje left BJP and joined Congress

Claimant: Facebook User

Fact Check: Fake
