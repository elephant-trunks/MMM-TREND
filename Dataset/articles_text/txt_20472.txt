Fact Check:

We reverse searched the video and found that the circulated part of the video starts off from 36 mins which can be seen on the official YouTube channel of High Court Karnataka.

We found that the lawyer was stripped by the Karnataka High Court for concealing material facts in a commercial appeal.

We also found only two judges in the video, but in hijab Controversy, the assigned judges are three.

Conclusion:

The video of the incident was shared by linking it to the hijab controversy.

Claim Review: viral video of CJI Karnataka is of hijab controversy

Claimed by: social media users.

Fact Check: Misleading.
